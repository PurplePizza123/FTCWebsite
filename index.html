About Me

I'm Ian, an eleventh-grade student with a passion for technology that knows no bounds. My heart beats for the exciting world of FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) robotics, where innovation meets competition. When I'm not tinkering with robots or strategizing for the next big match, you'll find me immersed in the intricate world of programming, turning lines of code into functional, creative solutions.

As a high school junior, I'm constantly pushing the boundaries of what I can learn and create. This website is a window into my journey through the realms of robotics and software development. Here, you'll find projects I've worked on, insights I've gained, and the aspirations that drive me forward in this ever-evolving field.

Join me as I navigate the challenges of FTC, share my programming adventures, and explore the endless possibilities that technology offers. Whether you're a fellow robotics enthusiast, a coding aficionado, or simply curious about what the next generation of tech innovators is up to, I'm excited to share my experiences with you!

Some of my goals include excelling in my AP Computer Science class and pre-calculus studies. I am passionate about pursuing a career in computer science. In addition to technology, I also enjoy chess and Nintendo games, especially those on the Switch and the classic NES. The Konami Code holds a special place in my heart, and "Escape" is a great start!
